Multi‑Stage Purification, Explained

See how True Purity delivers safe, balanced, great‑tasting water through a proven 6‑stage process.

100% Purity Balanced Taste ISO Certified

1 RO (Reverse Osmosis)

How it works: High‑precision RO membrane removes heavy metals, harmful impurities, and dissolved salts.

Why it’s good: Delivers clean, safe water free from dangerous contaminants.

2 UV (Ultraviolet)

How it works: UV light neutralizes bacteria and viruses—no chemicals required.

Why it’s good: Adds a microbiological safety layer while preserving natural taste.

3 UF (Ultrafiltration)

How it works: UF traps fine particles and cysts while allowing beneficial minerals to pass.

Why it’s good: Safe water even during low‑pressure or power‑cut conditions.

4 TDS (Total Dissolved Solids) Control

How it works: Smart TDS adjustment balances salts and minerals for taste.

Why it’s good: Perfect, consistent taste that’s just right for health.

5 Copper Enrichment

How it works: Adds copper traces, known for supporting good health.

Why it’s good: Supports immunity and natural wellness.

6 Alkaline Balance

How it works: Adjusts water pH for reduced acidity and smoother taste.

Why it’s good: Promotes better hydration, digestion, and overall balance.
